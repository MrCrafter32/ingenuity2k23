  • Hi! Are you new here?

    Seemed like it. Do you want me to give a tour? It wouldn't be a problem, honestly.

    I'll be more than happy to do so.

    It's been so long since we saw someone like you anyways. Would be a nice change of pace.

    Oh sorry, I meant that in the best way possible. You look different from everyone else here.

    No, nothing about the way you dress. It's just your aura, it feels concrete, defined, in complete contrast to our surroundings. We arose from rubble, perhaps would die here as well.

  • Ah, don't mind me, just blabbering. Old habit and I'm a creature of habit. These temples, you see and those halls over there, made of pure validation. Yeah, turns out not as strong of material as they had proclaimed. One crack and 'boom'! Don't worry, I know how this looks, the further we walk in the more old these arches seem, but they really aren't. They are just a testament of the massacre we went through.

    Oh, please don't be scared. Trust me, it's an interesting place, much like the gardens of underworld, one taste and you can never get enough. You know, once i was like you, young and beautiful, but time, it takes a toll on everyone. It's like a relationship which is free of your consent, you have no right over it, but to hopelessly surrender.

    When i was your age, this place was no less than a dystopia, with mansions forever lit, gardens ever-blooming, dances everywhere and a chirp in the air. so fresh, so kind. and then, the tragedy struck, if we can call it that.

  • The battle of reality.

    The singing of the birds now replaced with wails and gold-glittered ceilings with crumbling arches. Our glasses were too far from being full. Barely any water anywhere, raw commotion everywhere.

    But, one fine day, a group of young adults, found their way here, accidentally, of course, much like yourself, I'm supposing. They saw transcendence in this destruction and paved a way through our rust covered illusions.

    Sitting on a sidewalk, they started a symphony, grasping everyones' attention emotionally. The music was literal to our ears and the melodies had never been sweeter, a mixture of nostalgia and melancholy.

  • Don't cry, love! Your visit here has been just as fated.

    I don't know, nothing concrete, just the resonance of your being speaks to me.

    Don't take it so lightly, it’s not a tale to scare kids at night.

    Oh, one more thing, before you leave. I didn't catch your name, but can I make a guess?

    Is it Hope?

    Don't ask me how I know, I'll repeat the same as before. But anyhow, hello Hope! Welcome to Casimir.

About us

Popularly known as the cultural fest of NIIT university, it is not just that. Surrounded by the aravalis, away from the city pollution, but commanding the same impact. For those who work here, IngeNUity has succeeded the shackles of being a mere a fest, it is an emotion. No boundaries of teams bind anyone, somehow, at the end everyone works for everything. From the CR ka mahaul to fest ka bhoochal, IngeNUity is an emotional expedition which starts with a spectrum and ends with white colossal frenzy.
